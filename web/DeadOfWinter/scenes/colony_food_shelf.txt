食物供給堆

我們有 ${current_helpless_survivor+current_action_survivor} 位生存者在避難所，算了一下每天都需要用到 ${round_turn_food_need} 份食物。而在儲藏架上食物只有 ${current_colony_food} 份。

*temp area "colony"
*set user_stay_area area

*temp give_food 0
*temp trash_increase 1

*choice
  #提供食物
    你擁有 ${food_for_player} 份隨行食物，想要提供多少呢。

    *input_number give_food 0 food_for_player
    
    *set current_colony_food current_colony_food+give_food
    *set food_for_player food_for_player-give_food
    *set current_trash current_trash+trash_increase

    *gosub finish_give_food
  #返回避難所大廳
    *goto_scene colony
    
*label finish_give_food
你提供了 ${give_food} 份食物到食物供給堆上，且丟了 ${trash_increase} 份垃圾。
*choice
  #完成
    *goto start
